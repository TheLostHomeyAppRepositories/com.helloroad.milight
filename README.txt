Homey connects with your Mi•Light WiFi bridge to control the lights.

Supported bridges:
- First generation WiFi box
- Second generation WiFi iBox1
- Third generation WiFi iBox2

Mi•Light devices are also known as:
- EasyBulb
- AppLight
- AppLamp
- LEDme
- Dekolight
- Rocket LED
- s.LUCE
- iLight
- iBulb
- Kreuzer
